How HealingStreams work

Create an account

Download our mobile app or sign up on our website. You'll need to be registered at our partnered hospitals in Nigeria.

Order your medicine

Tell us which repeat prescriptions you need. Use the medicine search on our website, or use our mobile app to scan the barcode on a medicine packet.

Your Doctor Approves

We'll get in touch with your GP to confirm your order. Once they've checked it's correct, they'll send us an electronic prescription.

Over to our Phamarcists

For deliveries, we'll prepare and post your medicine from our central pharmacy. If you're collecting at LloydsPharmacy, your medicine will be prepared in store.

Devlivery or Pick Up

Deliveries are sent by our tracked dispatch riders, using letterbox friendly packaging whenever possible. For collections, your chosen HealingStreams phamarcy will let you know when your medicine is ready.

Get Reminders

You'll get a reminder to order your next prescription 10 days before you're due to run out. Don't take your medicine regularly? Just order when it suits you.
